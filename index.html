Welcome

This is the home page of Fresh Flower, an add-on for Mozilla Firefox. We are currently in the process to place it in Mozilla's repository. We will update this page when it is available there so please stay tuned and thank you for your patience!

Purpose

Fresh Flower is designed to help Firefox users refresh frequently updated tabs at some interval, by examining each tab's title and matching it against a set of text strings. If the title contains any of the strings the tab's content is refreshed.

Privacy Policy

Fresh Flower does not record, collect, transmit, or modify user data. It does not change any setting of the Firefox browser, either. Users can easily disable or remove it from the browser's Add-on Manager page.

How to Use Fresh Flower

Upon installation the add-on puts a button on the browser's toolbar, comprising of an icon resembling a flower and a downward arrow to the right. You click on the flower icon to turn the refreshing on or off, and click on the arrow to open the interval menu.

To change the set of matching text strings, click on the "Matching Text" section at the end of the menu. Change the text strings in the new window and then click on "Update Matches" at the bottom right corner.
